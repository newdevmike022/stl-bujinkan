Since 2005, the Saint Louis Bujinkan Dojo has been a center for learning the traditional martial arts of Japan. We offer classes in Bujinkan Budo Taijutsu and are a fully licensed dojo through the Bujinkan Shidoshi-kai (instructors guild) based in Noda-shi, Japan. Often referred to as ninjutsu or taijutsu, our training is focused on the skills, strategies and philosophies refined through war by the ninja and samurai classes. Training is for ages 14 and older and taught in a non-competitive atmosphere. We accept new students year-round and are committed to providing the highest quality and most accurate representation of the training that can be found in Japan.
